Productiviti

Our flagship product for work automation. Fully configurable and adaptable to any industry and business process. Supports pre-defined or user-defined task routing. Bundles an electronic forms module that lets creation and management of business forms used in the work processes. Allows browser-based document scanning with OCR capabilities.

HashMemo

Automates the creation and distribution of correspondences, whether internal or external. Includes workflows for collaborative correspondence composition and responses, organization structure-based dissemination and template based generation. Supports integration with various digital signature products and email and snail-mail oriented distribution. Has an email plug-in for email correspondence registration.

HashDocs

A powerful document management system that can provide a structured repository for all your unstructured information. HashDocs boasts of a modern user interface that is simple for the users yet providing all common document management features including taxonomy, security & access control, content and metadata based searches, versioning and ad-hoc document workflows.

HashCapture

Simple yet feature-rich document capture product. Provides batch scanning, Optical character recognition(OCR), Barcode reading, template based document recognition, image assisted indexing and connectors to all popular ECM systems. Supports searchable PDF generation while releasing to the ECM repository.
